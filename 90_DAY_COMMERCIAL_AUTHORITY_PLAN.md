# Barrana 90-Day Commercial Authority Plan

Last updated: 2026-07-21

Status: proposed queue. Publication requires the evidence and owner gates in `CONTENT_AUTHORITY_SYSTEM.md`.

## Objective

Reduce buyer confusion, establish a coherent commercial service system, and create a small set of source-backed pages that help a business identify, scope, govern, and measure one useful workflow implementation.

## Priority sequence

1. Correct the core buyer journey and high-risk claims.
2. Consolidate service and proof architecture.
3. Publish decision-support content tied to real sales questions.
4. Measure qualified progression before increasing volume.

## Supporting operating work

### Days 1 to 30

- Correct Home, Start Here, Process, Contact, About, primary navigation, and footer.
- Decide the Discovery Stage public terms.
- Create the private evidence and permission register.
- Classify every case study as verified, illustrative, private, or remove.
- Establish CI, route, link, sitemap, schema, claim, and accessibility checks.
- Record pre-change Search Console, analytics, and qualified-enquiry baselines when authorized.

### Days 31 to 60

- Consolidate the service architecture into a small set of workflow systems.
- Retain only owner-approved industry guides.
- Redirect malformed, duplicated, and low-value location pages.
- Separate verified proof from illustrative designs.
- Publish weeks 3 through 7 only after evidence review.

### Days 61 to 90

- Consolidate knowledge, resource, glossary, playbook, benchmark, and insight hubs.
- Complete metadata, schema, internal links, sitemap, `robots.txt`, and `llms.txt` review.
- Publish weeks 8 through 12 as evidence and commercial approvals allow.
- Review 30-day behavior before increasing output.

## Measurement baseline

Record after the corrected release is live:

- Search Console indexed pages and errors.
- Non-branded clicks and queries by buyer problem.
- Home to workflow CTA progression.
- Form starts and confirmed submissions.
- Qualified scheduling, if applicable.
- Workflow planner completion and exit points.
- Service, workflow, industry, and governance page engagement.
- Sales questions and objections that remain unanswered.
- Lead quality and fit, not only lead volume.
