# Barrana Commercial Authority Content System

Last updated: 2026-07-21

## Purpose

Barrana content exists to help a buyer make a better implementation decision and move toward a well-scoped workflow conversation. Publishing volume is not the objective. Each page must clarify the operational problem, possible system, human decisions, evidence, controls, dependencies, measurement, and next action.

Barrana is the commercial canonical source. IkramRana.com remains the personal authority source. Real Life AI remains Ikram Rana's practical AI publication.

## Content lanes

1. Workflow problem and implementation guides.
2. Service and integration explainers.
3. Industry workflow guides.
4. Buyer due-diligence, privacy, governance, and measurement guides.
5. Illustrative system designs with visible assumptions and human controls.
6. Verified implementation evidence with retained proof and publication permission.
7. Source-backed updates on meaningful changes in business AI adoption.

## Required content test

Every substantive item must answer:

1. What observable business problem exists before AI?
2. What workflow boundary is being discussed?
3. Does AI belong here?
4. What could improve?
5. What could get worse?
6. What decisions remain human?
7. What information, tools, permissions, and owners are involved?
8. What happens on uncertainty, error, or exception?
9. How will the full workflow be measured?
10. What should the reader do first?

## Evidence workflow

Before drafting, assign each factual claim one class:

- Verified public.
- Owner confirmed, evidence private.
- Dated use only.
- Illustrative scenario.
- Needs verification.
- Private record only.

Illustrative material must say: `Illustrative scenario, not a client result.`

Keep the following in a private evidence record when proof is not public:

- Source or engagement identifier.
- Exact claim or quotation.
- Measurement definition and period.
- Baseline and comparison method.
- Evidence location and owner.
- Attribution and publication permission.
- Approved public wording.
- Review or expiry date.

## Intake record

Create one GitHub issue form for new or updated content. Capture:

- Business question.
- Buyer and workflow.
- Barrana commercial position.
- Ikram Rana method or opinion, when relevant and attributed.
- Evidence links and claim classes.
- Assumptions and illustrative labels.
- Human decisions and escalation.
- Privacy, security, contractual, or regulated-industry risk.
- Related pages and consolidation impact.
- Intended CTA.
- Measurement plan.
- Owner, reviewer, priority, and review date.

## Page requirements

Each retained route needs:

- Audience and intent.
- Direct answer near the top.
- Unique value and relationship to other pages.
- Evidence class.
- Workflow, decisions, exceptions, controls, and measurement.
- Limits and poor-fit conditions.
- Accurate title, description, canonical, schema, and internal links.
- Content owner and review date.
- One stage-appropriate CTA.

## Publishing states

- Planned.
- Brief ready.
- Drafted.
- Evidence reviewed.
- Owner approved.
- Merged.
- Deployed.
- Live verified.
- Scheduled for review.
- Consolidated or retired.

Do not call a draft, approval, or merge published.

## Publishing rhythm

- Weekly: one strong guide, answer, or meaningful page update when there is a real buyer question.
- Every two weeks: improve one retained service, workflow, or industry page.
- Monthly: publish or update one governance, privacy, due-diligence, or measurement asset.
- Quarterly: review entity facts, offers, proof, cornerstone pages, important sources, crawl files, internal links, and the next topic queue.

Update a useful existing page when a new URL would create duplication.

## Distribution relationship

One approved topic may produce:

1. A canonical Barrana page or update that explains the commercial implementation decision.
2. An Ikram Rana LinkedIn post that introduces the point of view.
3. A Real Life AI or Substack note when the topic belongs in its broader educational role.
4. Supporting company social material using verified Barrana facts.

Cross-link with correct entity and author roles. Do not duplicate IkramRana.com essays page for page.

## Quality and release gates

Before merge:

- The business question is real and specific.
- The answer is original, useful, and understandable.
- Claims have evidence classes.
- Illustrative and verified proof cannot be confused.
- Sources are primary and current where the subject changes.
- Human judgment and failure paths are explicit.
- Public copy contains no em dashes.
- Prohibited claim scan passes.
- Type, build, route, link, sitemap, and schema checks pass.
- Desktop and mobile review pass.

Before publication:

- Owner-approved facts and commercial terms are unchanged.
- Deployment is separately authorized.
- The live page, metadata, canonical, schema, links, and event are verified.

## Measurement

Review monthly:

- Indexed canonical pages and indexation errors.
- Non-branded business-question queries.
- Visits and qualified progression from guides to the workflow CTA.
- Confirmed form submissions and scheduling where approved.
- Planner or calculator completion with privacy-safe event data.
- Citations, earned links, and relevant referring domains.
- Sales questions the content did or did not answer.
- Pages to correct, merge, expand, or retire.

Attention is not business impact. Connect content performance to qualified conversations and useful buyer behavior.
